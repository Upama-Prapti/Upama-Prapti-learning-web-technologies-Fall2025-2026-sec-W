How Z-Index Works

All three boxes use position: absolute and are positioned to overlap each other.

Box 1 (Red): z-index: 1 - Appears at the bottom layer

Box 2 (Blue): z-index: 2 - Appears in the middle layer

Box 3 (Green): z-index: 3 - Appears on top (highest z-index)

Higher z-index values appear on top of lower z-index values. Try swapping the z-index values in the CSS to see how the layering changes!
